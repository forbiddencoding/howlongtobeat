How long is The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt ?
When focusing on the main objectives, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is about 51½ Hours in length. If you're a gamer that strives to see all aspects of the game, you are likely to spend around 175 Hours to obtain 100% completion.
